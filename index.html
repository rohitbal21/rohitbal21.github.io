The Story How the Flame was Lit

Rohit studied at the University of California, San Diego where he had a great time playing in the sand and the sun. Most importantly he fell in love with spicy foods. Now each of his meals is spicier than the last (including breakfast). At restaurants, when they ask "mild, medium, or hot, sir?" he shows them the towel he brought from home.
